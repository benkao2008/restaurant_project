Top 5 Favorite Restaurants

1. Pho Tay Do

They are well-known with their signature dish called Bún bò Huế, a spicy Vietnamese beef rice vermicelli soup. Their other Pho dishes are not bad either but I always get their Bún bò Huế. The best homemade Bún bò Huế in the DFW area.

What is Bun Bo Hue

2. Lawry's

Awesome prime rib. Tender and juicy. I would highly recommend this place if you like steak in general. Their sides are also amazing. My personal favorite is the cream corn.

Lawry's Website

3. Fogo de Chão

The best Brazilian BBQ place around. Juicy meat and fresh salad bar. Their special saugage is one of the best. It is a must-try restaurant if you are a meat lover.

Fogo de Chão Website

4. LA Burger

This might seen like another burger joint, but don't let that fool you. They make their burger with their own twist by adding Bulgogi (Korean BBQ beef) inside of their bugers. K-style fries is definitely my favorite dish from there.

Full menu from LA Burger

5. Beijing Brothers

Best Beijing duck in the DFW area. They also hand pull their noodles right in front of you and put them in the duck broth. They also make great Chinese dumplings, especially the vegetable green dumplings

Beijing Brothers Website
